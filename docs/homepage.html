About WITH

A Brief Introduction to WITH

Women in Technological History (WITH) is a Special Interest Group within SHOT. Its membership includes both men and women interested in gender issues in the history of technology.

WITH has a two-fold mission:

  • To serve as a support network that encourages and energizes women in the profession
  • To act as an activist group promoting gender analysis in the history of technology
