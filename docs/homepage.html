About WITH

A Brief Introduction to WITH

Women in Technological History (WITH) is a Special Interest Group within SHOT. Its membership includes people interested in gender issues in the history of technology.

  • Serve as a support network that encourages and energizes women in the profession.
  • Promote gender analysis in the history of technology.
