About WITH

A brief narration of WITH

WITH (Women in Technology) is a growing archive dedicated to preserving the histories, experiences, and contributions of women in technological spaces. It brings together oral histories, photographs, documents, and institutional memory into a searchable, living collection.

Search first · less noise · faster decisions

Make women in technology history easier to browse and trust.

Start with search, move into a featured collection, then browse a clean set of archive cards. Secondary information stays out of the way.
